## **About this role**

As Yubo continues to scale, we’re entering a new phase of product ambition, especially within our **Engagement & Monetization squad**, whose mission is to continuously experiment and improve our core business metrics.

Following the rollout of our 2026 Tech organization and prepare for the future. We’re looking for a **Senior Backend Engineer** to join our **Backend Experience team**, reporting directly to Armand (Engineering Manager).

You will be embedded in a **multi-disciplinary squad** (Mobile Engineers, Product Manager, Designer, Data Analyst), alongside two backend engineers to help you ramp up fast.

Your role?

Set the technical direction and deliver backend systems that enable **continuous experimentation at scale**, rolling out services and business logic for millions of users while tracking interactions reliably and safely.

## **Your responsibilities**

*   **Define technical direction** for backend initiatives within the Engagement & Monetization squad.

*   Translate product and monetization goals into actionable backend specifications.

*   Deliver KPI-impacting backend changes that directly contribute to core business metrics.

*   Make sound trade-offs between speed, quality, scalability, and product outcomes, documenting decisions when needed.

*   Ensure production readiness (testing, resilience, observability, operational excellence).

*   Support experimentation by building measurable, well-instrumented, safe-to-iterate systems.

*   Improve tracking and observability to reliably attribute experiment impact

*   Contribute to backend codebase improvements (e.g., TypeScript migration)

*   Mentor engineers through reviews, pairing, and design feedback.

*   Create alignment across squads and proactively manage cross-team dependencies.

*   Strengthen documentation, specs, and onboarding materials to increase squad effectiveness.
