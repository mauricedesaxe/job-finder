## Job Description

**The Role**

As a Senior Go Engineer on the RPC Platform team at Quicknode, you will design, build, and scale the critical infrastructure that powers high-performance blockchain RPC services used by developers and enterprises globally. This role operates at the intersection of distributed systems, Web3 infrastructure, and developer experience, requiring deep technical ownership and a strong bias toward execution.

**What You’ll Do**

*   Design, build, and optimize high-throughput, low-latency backend services in Go.

*   Architect distributed systems.

*   Own end-to-end delivery from design through production.

*   Improve uptime and reduce latency across services.

*   Collaborate with Product, Infrastructure, and DevEx teams.

*   Debug and resolve complex production issues.

*   Drive engineering standards and best practices.

**What You’ll Bring**

*   6–10 years of experience building and operating backend or distributed systems in high-scale production environments, aligning with P4 expectations.

*   Strong proficiency in Go, with a demonstrated ability to quickly adopt new languages and frameworks as needed.

*   Deep experience designing and operating distributed systems, databases, and streaming architectures.

*   Proven track record of delivering scalable, reliable systems with measurable impact on performance, availability, and developer experience.

*   Experience working with Web3, blockchain infrastructure, or high-throughput API platforms, including familiarity with RPC services, node infrastructure, or data streaming pipelines.

*   Strong problem-solving skills with the ability to debug complex, cross-system issues in production environments.

*   Demonstrated ability to operate autonomously, prioritize effectively, and deliver outcomes in fast-paced, ambiguous environments.

*   Excellent communication skills with experience collaborating across engineering, product, and customer-facing teams.
