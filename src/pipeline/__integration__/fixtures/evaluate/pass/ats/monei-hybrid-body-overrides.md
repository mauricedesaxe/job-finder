## **About MONEI**

MONEI is revolutionizing omnichannel payments for top brands across Southern Europe. Our award-winning serverless architecture even earned recognition as the AWS Startup Architecture of the Year (Iberia region)! With rapid global expansion underway, we're building the future of payment processing—and we want you on our team.

**The Role**

As a full-time Senior Backend Engineer, you'll join a small, cross-functional product team building the backend systems that power MONEI. You'll make high-impact architectural decisions, tackle complex challenges, and help mentor junior engineers—all in an agile, collaborative environment where your ideas truly matter.

## What You'll Do

*   **Build Robust Payment Solutions:** Develop backend services that ensure a seamless digital and physical payment experience.

*   **Shape Our Architecture:** Collaborate with seasoned engineers to define and implement scalable, serverless systems on AWS.

*   **Mentor & Lead:** Guide 1-2 junior developers, fostering their growth and ensuring the highest coding standards.

*   **Ship Fast with AI:** Leverage Cursor, Claude Code, and Codex to move quickly on non-trivial backend work without sacrificing quality.

*   **Drive Innovation:** Contribute to team discussions, propose improvements, and help steer the evolution of our tech stack and platform.

## Our Stack

*   **Language:** TypeScript + Node.js

*   **Compute:** AWS Lambda (serverless-first, not EC2)

*   **Data:** DynamoDB, OpenSearch

*   **Messaging:** SQS, SNS, Kinesis

*   **API:** GraphQL via AWS AppSync

*   **IaC:** CDK / Serverless Framework

## What You'll Need

*   **Experience:** 5-7+ years in a demanding software engineering role.

*   **Technical Skills:** Strong expertise in TypeScript and Node.js.

*   **AWS Mastery:** Proven experience building, testing, and deploying distributed, highly available serverless systems on AWS.

*   **Tooling & Methodologies:** Microservices, event-driven programming, and automated testing.

*   **Coding Fundamentals:** Strong coding skills without AI assistance, verified in our live coding interview. We encourage AI tools, but you must understand and critically evaluate what they produce—no blind vibe-coding.

*   **Communication:** Excellent English skills and the ability to work both independently and collaboratively in a remote setting.

## Bonus Points

*   Experience with fintech, payment platforms, or SaaS products.
