We’re looking for a Senior Full-Stack Product Engineer who can own delivery end-to-end,from frontend to backend and operate effectively in an AI-native development environment.

This is not a traditional developer role.

At Whiteshield, we are building products and systems for governments and large institutions, where speed, ownership, and engineering judgment matter. Our workflows are evolving rapidly with AI-from development to QA and delivery and we need engineers who are already comfortable operating in this way.

You should be able to:

*   Take a feature from idea→architecture→implementation→deployment

*   Move fast using AI tools, without compromising on quality

*   Work independently while collaborating closely with product and leadership

WHAT YOU WILL DO |

*   Own end-to-end feature development across frontend, backend, and data layers

*   Build production-grade applications using Next.js, TypeScript, and modern web stacks

*   Design and implement clean, scalable APIs and services

*   Work directly with product managers, designers, and stakeholders to shape solutions

*   Debug issues across frontend, backend, and infrastructure layers

*   Use AI tools (LLMs, agents, automation) to accelerate development and delivery

*   Contribute to a fast-moving, highly collaborative, remote team environment
