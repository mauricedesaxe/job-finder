**About the company**

Trust Wallet is the leading non-custodial cryptocurrency wallet, trusted by over 200 million people worldwide to securely manage and grow their digital assets. Our vision is to give individuals the freedom to own their assets, confidently participate in the future economy, and access opportunities that enhance their lives. Our mission is to be a trusted personal companion — helping users safely navigate Web3, the on-chain economy, and the emerging AI-powered future. With support for over 10 million assets across 100+ blockchains, Trust Wallet offers a seamless, multi-chain experience backed by industry-leading self-custody technology, a vibrant community, and a growing ecosystem of partners.

Trust Wallet is the world’s most inclusive, chain-agnostic, self-custody wallet serving tens of millions of users globally.

We are building **Trust OS** — our internal AI platform powering intelligent features, automation, and next-generation user experiences across mobile, extension, and backend systems.

We are looking for a **Senior Full Stack Engineer** to take ownership of **Trust OS**, continue its development, and evolve it into a core capability of the wallet.

## **What You’ll Do**

*   Own and evolve **Trust OS** — our AI platform enabling agents, workflows, and integrations across wallet features

*   Build AI-powered experiences across the stack (mobile, web, backend)

*   Design and implement backend services in **Go** and integrate with LLMs and AI systems

*   Develop frontend and mobile features using **React / React Native**

*   Build and scale AI workflows (agents, memory, RAG, context management)

*   Work closely with Product and Leadership to shape **AI-driven roadmap and features**

*   Stay ahead of **crypto and AI trends** and translate them into product opportunities

*   Contribute to **on-chain features** (swaps, discovery, security, recommendations) powered by AI

*   Ensure performance, security, and reliability of AI systems in production

## **What We’re Looking For**

*   Strong full stack experience (frontend + backend systems)

*   Hands-on experience with **Typescript**, and preferably **Go/React Native**

*   Solid experience working with **LLMs / AI systems** (agents, prompts, RAG, etc.)

*   Strong system design and architecture skills

*   Deep interest and experience in **crypto / web3**

    *   Wallets, DeFi, on-chain data, or trading systems

    *   Strong awareness of current trends (AI + crypto, on-chain analytics, security, MEV, etc.)

*   Product mindset — able to **propose ideas, not just implement**

*   Ability to operate in ambiguity and drive **0 → 1 initiatives**

*   Strong understanding of:

    *   Security (especially AI + wallet context)

    *   Performance and scalability

    *   API and distributed systems design
