# A quick run-down of the job

We are looking for a Senior Backend Engineer with a dedicated focus on integrating Hyperliquid functionalities into our API platform. This role is backend-only—no frontend or design work required—letting you shape core integrations at the infrastructure layer. The ideal candidate is excited about Hyperliquid and its protocols, eager to help us distribute programmatic trading features (e.g., TWAP, perps) to the broader DeFi ecosystem through performant backend systems.

**Please note: You must be located in the EMEA region. Your application will not be considered otherwise.**

# What you'll do

*   Extend [LI.FI](http://li.fi/)’s API stack to include Hyperliquid ecosystem projects and feature sets

*   Design, build, and maintain backend services using **Node.js and TypeScript**

*   Work with **MongoDB, Redis, and MySQL** to support high-throughput, low-latency trading workflows

*   Collaborate closely with the Expansion PM and other backend engineers to improve integrations, routing, and user flows, and help evaluate new opportunities for feature expansion

*   Ensure robust, high-quality backend code with a strong focus on scalability, performance, and reliability for same-chain and cross-chain trading

*   Participate in code reviews, system design discussions, and technical documentation

# What makes a good candidate

*   **5+ years of experience in crypto**, ideally building or integrating production-grade DeFi or trading systems

*   **Strong backend engineering background** with hands-on experience in **Node.js and TypeScript**

*   **Database experience** with **MongoDB, Redis, and MySQL** in performance-sensitive backend environments

*   **Hyperliquid architecture familiarity:** Understanding of builder codes (spot, perps), HIP-3, precompiles, and HyperEVM at a design level, with the ability to keep up with evolving documentation

*   **Hyperliquid focus:** Genuine interest in the Hyperliquid ecosystem; prior projects, integrations, or self-driven exploration are highly valued

*   **Orderbook fundamentals:** Solid understanding of orderbook mechanics and comfort working with programmatic trading features

*   **EVM & integration patterns:** Experience building backend systems that integrate with EVM-based protocols; frontend or modal design work is not expected

*   **Independence:** Ability to drive integrations and propose new features with minimal oversight while collaborating effectively in a remote-first team

*   **Cross-chain experience:** Background working with bridges or backend infrastructure supporting multi-chain environments is a strong plus

**Bonus:** Familiarity with Solana, Bitcoin, or mobile backend development.

# How we hire

We prioritize candidates who show genuine excitement for Hyperliquid and demonstrate technical familiarity with its protocols. Expect conversations where you can showcase prior Hyperliquid-related projects and discuss ideas for future integration work.
