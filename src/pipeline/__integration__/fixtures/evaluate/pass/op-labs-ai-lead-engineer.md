**What are the role responsibilities?**

*   Partner Across the Organization

    *   Work closely with Data and business teams to understand workflows, identify pain points, and find high-impact opportunities for AI-driven automation and efficiency.

    *   Turn business problems into clear AI use cases.

*   Build & Prototype AI Solutions

    *   Design and iterate on practical AI tools such as copilots, agents, and automated workflows using modern GenAI techniques. Write clean, reliable code in Python and Golang to support these solutions.

*   Scale to Production

    *   Partner with engineering and data teams to turn prototypes into secure, scalable, production-ready systems.

*   Serve as an Internal AI Advisor

    *   Guide teams on AI capabilities, responsible use, and best practices. Mentor others on design, coding standards, and effective AI tools.

*   Drive Adoption

    *   Pilot new solutions with teams, gather feedback, iterate quickly, and support rollout to ensure real business impact.

*   Ensure Responsible AI

    *   Work with Security, Legal, and Compliance to meet privacy, security, and ethical standards.

*   Measure Impact

    *   Define success metrics and track outcomes such as:

        *   Time saved

        *   Cost reduction

        *   Increased speed and productivity

        *   Revenue impact

        *   Risk reduction

*   Stay Current

    *   Continuously explore new AI models, tools, and practices to bring practical innovations into the business.
