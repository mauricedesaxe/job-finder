## Description

As an Infrastructure Engineer, you'll build and deploy the computational infrastructure that powers Boundless including the compliance infrastructure for onchain finance, enabling transaction confidentiality, role-based access, and selective disclosure, delivered as a fully managed service that deploys across any public blockchain AND the world's largest decentralized proving network, orchestrating ZK proofs across every major blockchain. This role demands strong cloud-native infrastructure skills, with opportunities to go deep on bare-metal and GPU optimization for those interested.

**What You'll Do**

*   Cloud-Native Infrastructure: Architect highly available, auto-scaling infrastructure across multiple regions and cloud providers (AWS, GCP), meeting the reliability and compliance guarantees a managed financial service demands
*   Multi-Tenant Deployments: Build the infrastructure patterns that let us deploy across any public blockchain our customers operate on, with isolation and security appropriate for regulated environments
*   Release Management: Own deployment pipelines and release schedules for customer-facing environments, ensuring zero-downtime upgrades and safe rollouts across production tenants
*   Cost Optimization: Implement strategies to minimize infrastructure costs while maintaining performance, including spot instance management, workload placement between on-prem and cloud, and resource scheduling.

## Requirements

*   5+ years of infrastructure/DevOps experience operating large-scale production systems
*   Deep expertise in Kubernetes, Docker, and container orchestration at scale
*   Strong proficiency with at least one major cloud platform (AWS/GCP), including multi-region and auto-scaling architectures
*   Proficiency in infrastructure-as-code tools (Terraform, Ansible, Pulumi)
*   Strong Linux systems administration skills
*   Experience releasing complex software, including building and packaging Docker images, binaries, and maintaining distribution channels
*   Track record of managing mission-critical, high-throughput systems
*   Strong infrastructure-as-code background in heterogeneous environments
*   Proficiency in at least one common scripting or programming language (Python, Bash, TypeScript, Go, etc.)

N**ice to Have**

*   Experience with GPU computing infrastructure (CUDA, bare-metal optimization, kernel tuning)
*   Experience operating cryptocurrency mining or ML training infrastructure at scale
*   Familiarity with ZK proof generation or blockchain infrastructure
*   Experience with on-premises data center operations
*   Knowledge of network optimization and topology design
*   Experience with multi-region, globally distributed systems
*   Proficiency in Rust or low-level systems programming
