Bjak is focused on providing access to affordable and sustainable financial services for people in ASEAN. Headquartered in Malaysia, Bjak is the largest insurance portal in Southeast Asia. Our main portal, [Bjak.com](http://bjak.com/), helps millions find the insurance policy with the best value and highest coverage for them. Our investments in technology such as Custom API, trading systems and data science is to enable easy access to financial services that were previously inaccessible or difficult to understand. Join us on this exciting journey as we expand our operations in Malaysia. We're looking for dynamic individuals who share our vision and want to contribute to the next chapter of Bjak's success.

*   Develop high-quality backend systems using NodeJS, adhering to best coding practices.

*   Develop high-quality frontend applications using ReactJS / NextJS, adhering to best coding practices.

*   Analyze existing code and identify opportunities for enhancement and optimization.
