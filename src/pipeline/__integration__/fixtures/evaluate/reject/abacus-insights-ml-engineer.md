**About the role**

As a Senior AI Engineer, you’ll be at the center of how Abacus Insights builds, scales, and delivers AI‑powered products. This is a high‑impact role where you’ll design and deploy production‑grade GenAI systems, influence our AI strategy, and help shape the future of intelligent healthcare data platforms.

You’ll work hands‑on across the full AI lifecycle while serving as a technical leader and mentor for globally distributed teams. Partnering closely with product, platform, and engineering leadership, you’ll help ensure our AI platforms are scalable, reliable, and ready to support rapid business growth.

**Your day to day**

*   Lead the development of applied AI and GenAI capabilities across our products, including LLMs, agents, RAG, and AI‑driven data solutions
*   Design, build, and operate end‑to‑end ML/AI pipelines—from experimentation to production, monitoring, and continuous improvement
*   Create scalable backend systems, observability, and evaluation frameworks that power GenAI across the company
*   Collaborate with AI, data, platform, and product teams to deliver AI solutions that meaningfully improve customer outcomes
*   Influence architecture and technical direction while mentoring engineers and raising the bar for engineering excellence
*   Drive innovation while balancing delivery, quality, and operational stability in a fast‑moving environment

**What you bring to the team**

*   Bachelors Degree in Computer Science, Engineering, Data Science with demonstrated depth in applied AI and large-scale systems, or relevant professional experience.
*   7–10 years of software and data engineering experience, including 3–5 years building ML/AI or GenAI systems
*   2 Years of experience with Databricks and modern data/AI platforms
*   Deep hands‑on experience with LLMs, embeddings, agents, RAG, MCP, and modern AI evaluation techniques
*   Strong Python skills and experience with ML frameworks like PyTorch or TensorFlow
*   Experience designing scalable, distributed systems for data‑intensive workloads
*   A track record of leading complex projects, influencing technical decisions, and mentoring engineers
*   A passion for building reliable, high‑quality AI systems with real‑world impact

**What we would like to see, but not required:**

*   Mosaic AI, Snowflake, FHIR, Healthcare Data exposure
