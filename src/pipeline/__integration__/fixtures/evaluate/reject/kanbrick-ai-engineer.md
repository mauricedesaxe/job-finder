## Description

### About Kanbrick

Kanbrick is a purpose-driven, long-term investment partnership built to empower people and organizations to reach their full potential. We partner with family- and founder-owned businesses that have strong moats and untapped potential, helping them grow through a hands-on focus on people, culture, and operating systems. We think in decades, not quarters-and as seasoned operators and true partners, we roll up our sleeves to build enduring companies, brick by brick.

### Role Overview

Kanbrick is building an AI and technology enablement capability across the firm and our partner companies. This role is the first dedicated hire against that effort. You will identify where AI agents create the most operational leverage across Kanbrick and our partner companies, design and deploy the workflows to capture that leverage, and manage those systems on an ongoing basis.

You will work across two surfaces: internally, building the AI tooling, skills ecosystem, and automation infrastructure that makes the Kanbrick team more productive; and externally, embedding with partner company teams to map their highest-value workflows, deploy agent-driven automation, and coach them through adoption. What you build in the first year becomes the template for how AI operations works across the entire portfolio.

This role reports to the VP of Technology and AI and is a member of the Kanbrick Business System (KBS) operating team.

### Key Responsibilities

**Productizing &Deploying Specialist Tools**

Guide our KBS team leaders through tool development and help them “go the final mile.”That means:embedding a tool directly into a partner company's day-to-day workflow and supporting adoption, turning a promising prototype into a formal, structured, repeatable asset that scales across the portfolio and serving as a technical partner to our team. You'll map data flows across business systems, connect existing platforms (CRMs, ERPs, financial tools) via MCP servers and APIs, deploy agents into production with clear human-in-the-loop interfaces, and manage them on an ongoing basis-tracking KPIs, running evals after model or data changes, and iterating on performance.

**AI Tooling & Skills Ecosystem**

Make our team 10x more productive. Formalize and hone Kanbrick's Skills library, accelerate the use of “AI coworking tools”,and build toward a full KBS plug-in suite-a set of tools every operator across the portfolio reaches for by default. Own the build and curation of the internal AI-enabled projects, tools, and skills repository; turn ad hoc experiments into repeatable, version-controlled, distributable assets; and maintain a partner-facing skills library with documentation that lets teams browse, install, and use tools independently.

**Drive Enterprise Software Development**

Beyond skills repositories and AI enablement tooling,a handful of workflows will warrant graduation into “Enterprise-Level” software-durable, production-grade systems that scale across our team and select partner companies.You'll take end-to-end ownership of these builds: pressure-testing which problems justify enterprise investment, scoping architecture and data models, partnering with internal stakeholders and outside engineering resources where needed, and shepherding the system from prototype through deployment, adoption, and ongoing operation.
